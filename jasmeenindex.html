What is Cybercrime

Cybercrime is defined as any type of illegal activities using technology.
These cybercriminals create a computer virus and trojan programs
(Trojan Horse hides malware from what appears to be a normal file.
There is a wide variety of Trojan viruses on the Internet that can perform a variety of tasks).

 

Cybercrime can:

  • Take control of the user’s computer, steal data and inserts more malware to the victim’s computer.

  • Stealing bank account access codes.

  • Illegally use an infected computer- to develop and run:
    • - Spams campaigns ( A spam operation that commands a certain number of messages to send to a series of victim email addresses is called bulk)
    - Blackmailing operation
    - Distributes Networks attract ( DDoS) - is a cyber-attack where cyber-criminals take malicious action against a target by sending a vast amount of traffic to a single website or machine, making it unavailable to their intended users.
