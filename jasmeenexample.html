Examples/Types of Cybercrime


Phishing

It is a method of social engineering, where criminals get
sensitive information, such as usernames and passwords.
Phishing is done by sending electronic mail, mail with links to hacked websites that look like real ones.
Once you login to the fake website and your login details are stored in the attacker's computer.
Then they use your credit cards, band accounts or other services.
 



Ransomware

It is a software that infects your computer and displays messages demanding a fee to be paid for your system to work.
Malware- It is a contraction of malicious software.
Its intention is to damage the device, steal data and cause a mess. Viruses, trojans, ransomware are a different kind of malware.



Social engineering

It is one of the most classic types of cybercrime.
It involves manipulating to get valuable information that later is used to log illegally
into the private protected system. The motivation behind
this is often to steal money and financial data.



Cyberstalking

Is a form of cyberbullying in which an individual
attempt to threaten or harass other
people by using computer systems.
